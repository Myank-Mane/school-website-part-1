Swami Vivekananda International School and JR College

Founder-Yogesh Patel

Swami Vivekanand International School and Jr College situated at Kandivali,Mumbai was established in 1998. The school is affiliated to Indian Council of Secondary Education (ICSE Board). The school follows an experiential learning program integrating principles of Howard Gardner’s Multiple Intelligences in its pedagogy. > We believe that each student is unique and has the ability to display excellence in one or more skills. Our experiential learning philosophy ensures that students not only understand academic concepts but are also able to apply it in the world around them. Emphasis is placed to ensure that each and every student participates in extracurricular activities, sports and performing arts, thus providing opportunities discover their own talents and interests. This invokes the students curiosity to ask questions and their confidence to express themselves in every aspect of life. The school believes in teaching by love and also places emphasis on development of social values including - equality, tolerance and dignity. At Swami Vivekanand International School and Jr College, we apply rigorous standards to ensure that we have the best of teachers who engage in active professional development so that they can lead the children by example and prepare them for a bright future. The methodology governing the curricular and extracurricular activities in the School revolve around the well-established two dicta encompassing the philosophy of education: "Liberty is the first condition of growth" (as expressed by Swami Vivekanand). Mahatma Gandhi's famous expression - "freedom, fearlessness, mutual love and respect constitute the natural atmosphere of education." Quality Policy We, at Swami Vivekanand International School- Are committed to provide education at school level in accordance with the mandatory requirements. Impart knowledge and all round exposure to students that enables them to face the challenges of the competitive world. Continuously upgrade ourselves to meet the expectations of parents and students. Motivate the staff to work as a team and update their skills to ensure the best professional standards.





Campus

1. Classrooms Commodious well ventilated room with big windows, age appropriate & comfortable furniture, bulletin boards and Audio- Visual interactive smart boards form a typical classroom at the school.

2. Canteen Value education starts here with children being encouraged to share their food, cultivate tastes and tolerate cultural differences. Needless to add, the variety available is nutritious and wholesome, a much needed requirement for energetic and active students.

. Counselling The Department has 'Counsellors' to help the children with behavioral problems and 'Special Educators' to guide the children with learning disorders. This ensures that every child is kept in the mainstream. In a nutshell, it acts as a leveller for the children with a physical or mental handicap. In the long run, it will induce a positive attitude towards life in children, especially those who are handicapped.

4. Transport A fleet of buses plies across the city to provide safe, comfortable and timely transportation to students of the school. Every bus has a fire extinguisher and a first aid box. The students are escorted and assisted by a lady attendant in each bus.
5. Library “Reading is synonymous to growth”… This silent sanctuary for research is our pride and joy. Our reading programs are integrated with the core curriculum making our students more articulate and broadening their vision. We encourage our students to spend a considerable amount of their time in this haven of knowledge.
6. Computer Lab This section of the school keeps the children updated and connected with the world. Here they learn to use and apply modern software applicable to their grades and stay abreast with the political and financial arenas globally. 7. Science Labs Well equipped Science Labs are an integral part of the learning process. Our Science laboratories are fully equipped to support the scientific temperaments of the child. This is where the curious minds of the students are put to test. 8. Play Ground The most sought after corporate trainer in the world is the playground. Here players are taught primary values and survival skills. Teamwork, fight fair, focus, consistency, strategy and most important of all – there is only one First Place. Our sporting faculty concentrates on these values while coaching the students in a variety of sports such as Football, Cricket, Volleyball, Martial Arts etc. 9. Multi Purpose Hall This room is our storehouse of memories. Auditions, practice sessions, plays, games, debates, competitions, dance classes, music lessons - its use is diverse and its nature dynamic. 10. Pen with Sand Play For young ones, the play pen is where they build their dream castles. A smart learning tool that develops motor skills of using hands and intelligence, the sand pit is also a very useful play option. 11. Security The school takes utmost care of the staff & students and in keeping with the same; Candid Cameras have been installed at the major points on the campus. The Public Announcement facility in all the rooms of the school enables quick and correct delivery of information collectively. Fire extinguishers are mounted at conspicuous locations in the corridors. A mock drill on Fire Fighting is conducted for students & staff annually. We have sufficient male and female personnel that maintain security within the school premises.
